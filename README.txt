MedCityAI

MedCityAI is a Rochester, Minnesota focused research platform that highlights recent
biomedical publications and provides AI-generated summaries to make research easy to understand and share. The site includes search, curated highlights, and article pages
with links back to original PubMed source. Information is sourced from National Library of Medicine and PubMed.
