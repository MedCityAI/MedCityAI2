Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Med City AI and our research platform

How can I access the research database?

Our platform provides free access to analyzed biomedical research from Rochester. Simply browse our research dashboard or use our search functionality to find relevant studies.

Can I collaborate with Med City AI?

We welcome collaborations with researchers, institutions, and organizations. Please reach out via email to discuss potential partnership opportunities.

How often is the database updated?

Our database is continuously updated with the latest research publications. New studies are analyzed and added to our platform regularly to ensure current information.

Do you provide API access?

We're working on API access for researchers and developers. Contact us to learn about early access opportunities and integration possibilities.
