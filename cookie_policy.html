Terms of Use Privacy Policy Cookie Policy

Good News! Med City AI currently does NOT use cookies, localStorage, or any other browser storage technologies. You can browse our site with confidence knowing we're not tracking you or storing data on your device.

1. Current Cookie Usage

Med City AI currently does NOT use cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage, or any other browser storage technologies. Our website operates entirely without storing data on your device. This means:

  • ✓ No tracking of your browsing behavior
  • ✓ No persistent storage of your searches or preferences
  • ✓ No third-party advertising or analytics cookies
  • ✓ No need to accept annoying cookie consent banners
  • ✓ Maximum privacy for all visitors

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to store information about your preferences, login status, or browsing behavior. Common types include:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period
  • First-Party Cookies: Cookies set by the website you're visiting
  • Third-Party Cookies: Cookies set by other domains (usually for advertising or tracking)

While many websites use cookies extensively, Med City AI has been designed to function fully without them.

3. Why We Don't Use Cookies

We believe in privacy by design. Our philosophy is to collect only what's absolutely necessary to provide our service. Since we can deliver full functionality without cookies, we choose not to use them. This approach offers several benefits:

  • Enhanced Privacy: No tracking or profiling of user behavior
  • Transparency: What you see is what you get—no hidden tracking
  • Simplicity: No need to manage cookie preferences or consent forms
  • Compliance: Fewer regulatory concerns related to privacy laws
  • Performance: Slightly faster page loads without cookie processing

4. Data We Do Collect (Server Logs)

While we don't use cookies, our web server automatically logs basic technical information when you visit the site. This is standard for all websites and is necessary for basic operation:

  • IP Address: Your device's internet address (automatically anonymized after a short period)
  • Browser Type: What browser you're using (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • Device Type: Desktop, mobile, or tablet
  • Access Time: When you visited the site
  • Referrer: What website sent you to us (if any)
  • Pages Viewed: Which pages you accessed during your visit

Important: This logging happens at the server level (not in your browser) and is temporary. These logs are used only for security, troubleshooting, and basic analytics. They cannot be blocked like cookies, as they're fundamental to how the web works.

5. Local Storage and Session Storage

In addition to traditional cookies, modern browsers support other storage mechanisms like localStorage and sessionStorage. We do not use these technologies either. Your browser's storage remains completely empty when you visit Med City AI.

6. Third-Party Cookies

Med City AI does not integrate any third-party services that use cookies on our domain. However, when you click links to external sites (such as PubMed or publisher websites), those external sites may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

External sites we link to:

  • PubMed (pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov): May use cookies according to NIH privacy policy
  • Publisher Websites: May use cookies for functionality and analytics
  • Social Media Platforms: May use cookies when you share content

We have no control over and are not responsible for cookies set by third-party websites. Please review their privacy policies for information about their cookie practices.

8. Your Privacy Controls

Since we don't use cookies, there are no cookie-related settings to manage on Med City AI. However, you can control cookies from other websites you visit through various methods:

8.1 Browser Settings

All modern browsers allow you to control cookie settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

8.2 Browser Extensions

Consider using privacy-focused browser extensions:

  • Privacy Badger (blocks tracking cookies)
  • uBlock Origin (blocks ads and trackers)
  • Cookie AutoDelete (automatically deletes cookies)

8.3 Private Browsing

Use your browser's private/incognito mode to browse without storing cookies:

  • Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+N (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+N (Mac)
  • Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+P (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+P (Mac)
  • Safari: Cmd+Shift+N (Mac)
  • Edge: Ctrl+Shift+N (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+N (Mac)

9. Legal Compliance

9.1 GDPR (European Union)

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) requires websites to obtain consent before using non-essential cookies. Since Med City AI doesn't use cookies, we're already compliant with GDPR cookie requirements.

9.2 CCPA (California)

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives users rights regarding their personal information. Since we don't use cookies to track users, most CCPA concerns related to tracking don't apply to our site.

9.3 ePrivacy Directive

The EU's ePrivacy Directive (Cookie Law) requires websites to inform users about cookies and obtain consent. Since we don't use cookies, this directive doesn't apply to Med City AI.

10. How to Verify We Don't Use Cookies

You can verify our no-cookie policy yourself using browser developer tools:

  1. Open your browser's developer tools (usually F12 key)
  2. Navigate to the "Application" tab (Chrome/Edge) or "Storage" tab (Firefox)
  3. Check the "Cookies" section—it should be empty for medcityai.com
  4. Check "Local Storage" and "Session Storage"—also empty

11. Questions About Cookies

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy or want to report a concern, please contact us through the Contact page.

12. Changes to This Policy

We will update this Cookie Policy if our practices change. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last revised. If we begin using cookies, we will update this policy in advance and implement appropriate consent mechanisms.
