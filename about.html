Our Purpose

MedCityAI was created to enhance visibility and accessibility of cutting-edge research emerging from Rochester, Minnesota's world-renowned medical institutions. By bringing together publications from Mayo Clinic, the University of Minnesota Rochester, and other leading research centers, we create a comprehensive view of the biomedical innovation happening in Med City.

Our platform leverages artificial intelligence to curate, analyze, and present research publications in an intuitive format. We transform the overwhelming volume of scientific literature into actionable insights, helping researchers, clinicians, and the community stay informed about the latest medical breakthroughs and discoveries.

Through real-time monitoring, intelligent categorization, and collaborative analytics, MedCityAI bridges the gap between research output and knowledge dissemination, fostering greater awareness of Rochester's significant contributions to global health and medicine.
