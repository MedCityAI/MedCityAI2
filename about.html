About MedCityAI

Transforming biomedical research discovery through AI-powered insights and real-time analytics for Rochester, Minnesota's thriving medical community.

Our Vision

MedCityAI bridges the gap between cutting-edge biomedical research and the Rochester medical community. We believe that timely access to research insights can accelerate discovery, improve patient outcomes, and foster collaboration.

Our platform leverages artificial intelligence to curate, summarize, and present the most relevant research publications from PubMed, specifically focused on work emerging from Rochester's world-class medical institutions.

By making research more accessible and digestible, we're empowering clinicians, researchers, and students to stay informed about the latest developments in their fields.

Meet the Team

NH

Nathan Huber, PhD

Founder & Chief Scientist

Dr. Nathan Huber has been an integral part of Rochester's medtech community for the past eight years. He began his academic journey studying physics and chemistry at Gustavus Adolphus College in Saint Peter, Minnesota, where he developed a strong foundation in analytical thinking and scientific methodology.

Nathan pursued his graduate studies at the prestigious Mayo Clinic Graduate School, where he earned his PhD in Biomedical Engineering. His doctoral research focused on advanced imaging technologies and their clinical applications, bridging the gap between engineering innovation and medical practice.

Currently, Dr. Huber serves as a CT Clinical Scientist for Philips Healthcare, where he works at the forefront of medical imaging technology development. He also holds the position of Adjunct Assistant Professor of Radiology at Mayo Clinic, where he mentors the next generation of researchers and clinicians.

His unique perspective, combining deep technical expertise with clinical insight, drives MedCityAI's mission to make biomedical research more accessible and actionable for the Rochester medical community.
